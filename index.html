company

vision | leadership

the problem

Today’s digital economy has created new challenges for organizations attempting to stay current and competitive. Managing and harnessing information is part art and part science, and organizations need the right tools in order to compete. Everyone is struggling to find and retain the talent that can execute on their strategy and too often they are forced to make do or outsource the need to an outside vendor.

the solution

We believe every organization has an opportunity to actualize its potential by directly confronting the disruptions it faces in the marketplace. That is why we started Branch Forward. We enable emergence into the digital age by helping organizations acquire the resources and knowledge they need to thrive in today’s economy.

We have a collective 30 years managing interdisciplinary teams tasked with developing new products and services in sectors facing dramatic technological disruption. We intimately understand the challenge of harnessing technology as a competitive advantage and can help you build strategic and tactical functions to adapt to and capitalize on disruptions happening in your industry.

enable

We offer consulting services that support organizations at various stages of development in Data Management and Analytics. Whether you have a strategy or assets you would like evaluated, or you would like help thinking of new approaches to the challenges you face, our consultants can help. Our enablement methodology consists of a three-fold approach, focused on defining and building the human resources and infrastructure to compete.

  1. Audit: Where are you today and where do you need to go?
  2. Build: Develop the teams and infrastructure that will get you there.
  3. Integrate: Integrate the function effectively with the rest of your organization.

We customize an approach that is tailored to your business needs and charges a competitive hourly rate.
