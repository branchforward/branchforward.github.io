our mission

We believe every organization must directly confront the ever-growing complexity of the digital marketplace. That is why we started Branch Forward. We enable organizations to compete by helping them acquire the knowledge they need to thrive in the digital economy today and well into the future.

our solutions

Our expertise is Digital Content Management and Analytics. Our goal is to enable your organization to create, enrich and evolve your digital fluency. We do this with the following solutions:

senteme

Intelligent eCommerce Analytics that summarize user sentiment and trends on your products as found in user-generated content across the web.

search optimization

Create a user experience that speaks the language of every user and finds the right result, every time.

content strategy

Define your vision, strategy and tactics for creating and maintaining competitive digital assets.

data design

Design ontologies, taxonomies and metadata architectures that enable effective search and find for every end user.

training and enablement

Help build and train your teams to create best-in-class content that evolves, keeping pace with industry trends and advances.

data transformation

Map content from one source to another and enhance and optimize it for each required use case.

our experience

We have deep expertise in Digital Content Management and Analytics and have served customers facing dramatic technological disruption for over a decade. Our experience spans all channels of eCommerce and a full range of verticals, from Industrial Supply and Medical to Retail and Aerospace.
